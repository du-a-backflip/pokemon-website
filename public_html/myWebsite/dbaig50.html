About Me: Dua

Hey, my name is Dua! I know how to speak both Urdu and English (although my English is definitely the better of the two). I'm currently learning Mandarin as well, but it's pretty hard. I have a lot of trouble translating the listening material - Chinese people talk so fast that it's hard for my brain to keep up.

Speaking of learning, my favorite class is Human Genetics. I like the teacher and biology, and since it's an elective, it's pretty chill and relaxed. When not doing schoolwork, my favorite type of activity to do is drawing. I like to draw roses - they are my favorite flower (and sunflowers too, but I don't know how to draw them). My favorite art supply for drawing are my mechanical pencils (Tombow Mono Graph 0.5), and my favorite song to listen to is Doja Cat's "Like That". I wish I had a Tombow mechanical pencil in pink or red, since those are my favorite colors (especially to wear). My favorite scarf is pink, and it's pretty cozy.
