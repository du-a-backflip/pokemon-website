时间 (shíjiān) - Time

UNITS OF TIME

  • 年 (nián) - year

  • 月(yuè) - month (needs measure word when expressing quantity, uses 个 (ge))

  • 号(hào) - day of the month

  • 星期(xīngqí) - week (needs measure word when expressing quantity, uses 个 (ge))

  • 周末(zhōumò) - weekend (needs measure word when expressing quantity, uses 个 (ge))

  • 小时(xiǎoshí) - hour (needs measure word when expressing quantity, uses 个 (ge))

  • 半小时(bànxiǎoshí) - half-hour, 30 minutes

  • 刻(kè) - quarter of an hour, 15 minutes

  • 分（钟）(fēn(zhōng)) - minute

  • 秒(miǎo) - second


DEFINITE TIME

  • 前年（qiánnián） - the year before the previous year; two years ago

  • 去年(qùnián) - last year

  • 今年(jīnnián) - this year

  • 明年(míngnián) - next year

  • 后年(hòunián) - the year after next year; two years later


  • 前天(qiántiān) - the day before yesterday; two days ago

  • 昨天(zuótiān) - yesterday

  • 今天(jīntiān) - today

  • 明天(míngtiān) - tomorrow

  • 后天(hòutiān) - the day after tomorrow; two days later


  • 上上个月(shàngshànggèyuè) - the month before last month; two months ago

  • 上个月(shànggèyuè) - last month

  • 这个月(zhègeyuè) - this month

  • 下个月(xiàgèyuè) - next month

  • 下下个月(xiàxiàgeyuè) - the month after next month; two months later


  • 上上星期(shàngshàngxīngqí) - the week before last week; two weeks ago

  • 上个星期(shànggèxīngqí) - last week

  • 这个星期(zhègexīngqí) - this week

  • 下个星期(xiàgèxīngqí) - next week

  • 下下个星期(xiàxiàgèxīngqí) - the week after next week; two weeks later


TIMES OF DAY

  • 现在(xiànzài) - now

  • 早上(zǎoshang) - in the morning; (early)morning

  • 中午(zhōngwǔ) - noon; midday

  • 下午(xiàwǔ) - afternoon

  • 晚上(wǎnshàng) - at the evening, at night


FREQUENCY

  • 总是(zǒng shì) - always

  • 平常(píngcháng) - frequently

  • 平时(píngshí) - frequently

  • 常常(chángcháng) - frequently

  • 几乎不(jīhūbù) - rarely

  • 很少(hěnshǎo) - rarely

  • 不从来(bùcónglái) - never
