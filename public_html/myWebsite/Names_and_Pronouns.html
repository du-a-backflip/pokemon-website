Pronouns

  • 我 (wǒ) - I, me

  • 你(nǐ) - you (informal/casual)
    • 您 (nín) - you (but formal - used to show respect)

  • 他(tā) - He

  • 她(tā) - She

  • 它(tā) - It

  • 我们(wǒ men) - we, us

  • 你们(nǐ men) - You (plural)
    • 您们(nín men) - You (plural, formal)

  • 他们(tā men) - Them (a group of men or boys, or mixed gendered people)

  • 她们(tā men) - Them (a group of women or girls)

  • 它们(tā men) - Them (a group of objects)


Names

  • 名字(ming zi) - name

  • 姓(xìng) - surname

  • 叫(jiào) - (v.) called

  • 你叫什么名字？ - What is your Name?
