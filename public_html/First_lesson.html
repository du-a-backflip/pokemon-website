第九课：买东西
Lesson 9: Buying Things

生词(sheng ci) - New Vocabulary

衣服（yi fu) - Clothing

穿（chuan) - (v) to put on; to wear

  • Note:THIS IS ONLY USED FOR CLOTHING, NOT ACCESSORIES.

TOPS
件(jian) - measure word for (clothing) tops and general pieces of clothing (衣服)

  • 衬衫(Chèn shān) - (n) shirt, formal shirt, blouse
  • 短袖(Duǎn xiù) - (n) short-sleved shirt
  • 体恤衫(Tǐ xùshān) - (n) T-shirt
  • 毛衣(Máo yī) - (n) sweater
  • 卫衣(wèi yī) - (n) hoodie
  • 外套(wài tào) - (n) coat
  • 大衣(dà yī) - (n) overcoat (the longer, fancier kind of coats)
  • 夹克(jiā kè) - (n) jacket (the shorter kind that only covers the torso)
  • 雨衣(Yǔ yī) - (n) raincoat

BOTTOMS
条(tiáo) - measure word for long, thin, things, like clothing+ bottoms

  • 裤子(kù zi) - (n) pants
  • 裙子(qún zi) - (n) skirt
