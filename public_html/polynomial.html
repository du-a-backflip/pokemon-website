My Favorite Polynomial

My favorite Polynomial is y=x^3+4x^2+18x+27. I like this polynomial because I think 3 is a pretty satisfying number(hence x being cubed). As they say "Good things come in threes" - like the flavors in Neopolitan icecream, and the components of a nucleotide (phosphate, base, and deoxyribose). I suppose my feelings towards three also extend to the number 27, as 27 is a multiple of 3 and is also a perfect cube(9 x 9 x 9). I also like the numbers 4 and 18, since they make me think of my birthday.
